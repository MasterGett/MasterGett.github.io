Welcome!

Creek House Farm is a family farm located in the Manchester area of Port Orchard, Washington. We raise over 20 varieties of organic, heirloom pumpkins and winter squash.

In October, we open the pumpkin patch to the public. Guests can select pumpkins straight from the field, experience multiple family-fun activities across the farm, and enjoy many opportunities for photos!

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our farm.
