Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you charge an entrance fee to visit?

A: Short answer: yes. Guests pay for entry when scheduling a visit. This is covered in more detail on the 'Farm Visits' page.

Q: Do you allow dogs on the farm?

A: Yes, so long as they behave well around small children and other animals, and remain on a leash for the duration of the visit.

Q: Is there anything that we are required to bring for our visit?

A: While nothing is necessarily 'required' to visit our farm, there are some things we would recommend, such as rubber boots and warm wear if the weather is likely to be cold and/or rainy.

Q: Do you accept cards for payment?

A: Yes. We accept all Visa and Mastercard credit/debit cards.
