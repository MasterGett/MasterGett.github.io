Birthday Parties

Birthdays are such a fun time and we thoroughly enjoy being a part of the celebration here at Creek House Farm. If you choose to have a birthday party here, you have the opportunity to participate in farm activities along with other farm guests. Please remember that your admission allows for a 90 minute farm visit (including your birthday celebration).

We do offer you the option of having a special place set-aside for your group, with our large picnic table and benches, as a place for your group to celebrate your birthday. You will need to supply/bring along your own decorations and paper products for your party, as well as cleaning up the area and disposing of your own trash, when you are finished. As we are a family farm, which attracts many young children, we ask that you do not include anything frightening or scary in your decorations. Typical children themes are acceptable, along with happy pumpkins, or autumn/harvest/farm themes. You are welcome to bring birthday refreshments for your party, but we do not allow alcohol on our farm.

There is no venue fee for having a birthday at our farm. An admission package must be purchased for each guest when you make your reservation. Please contact the farm prior to making your online reservation to confirm that our birthday area is available at the time you desire.
